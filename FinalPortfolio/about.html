My story starts in in New Jersey. At age seven I moved to England for two years, and shortly after spent the rest of my adolescence in the city of Hong Kong. I think its safe to say that I am a very international person.

Having grown up in various different environments, I have grown a love for culture and various forms of art. Being influenced and also impacted by so many important causes I have begun a journey of using art and design to bring attention to important human needs that we overlook everyday.
